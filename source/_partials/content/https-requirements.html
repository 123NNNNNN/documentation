Requirements for Automated Certificate Renewal

  • All A/AAAA records must point to a server that serves Pantheon's acme-challenge response (e.g., http://23.185.0.1/.well-known/acme-challenge/[variableDataHere]).
  • Authority Name Servers must serve mixed-case lookups.
  • Authority Name Servers must not fail CAA lookups.
  • CAA records must either 1) not exist for the domain and its parent domains or 2) authorize Let's Encrypt.
  • CNAMEs records must point to Pantheon's provided platform domain for the target environment. Subdomains such as www.example.com do not require A/AAAA records.
  • DNAMEs must not be used.

Please check this doc periodically as requirements may be added or removed.

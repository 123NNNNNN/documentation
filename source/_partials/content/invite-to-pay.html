1. From the organization's Dashboard, click Organizations, then select the Agency.

  2. Click the site's name to enter the Site Dashboard, then click Settings.

    • If the site has not added the Supporting Agency, the Settings modal will display a note with either the cardholder's name and card type, or the modal will display instructions regarding alerting the Site Owner.


      If this is the case, the Site Owner should follow the steps to transfer ownership and billing for the site. The Agency can continue these steps once ownership has been transferred.
